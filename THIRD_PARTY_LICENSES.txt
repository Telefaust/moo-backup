distribution (_internal\ directory). Their licenses are listed below.


paramiko 5.0.0
--------------
License: LGPL-2.1
Homepage: https://www.paramiko.org/
Source:   https://github.com/paramiko/paramiko

Moo-backup uses paramiko as a dynamically-loaded library for SSH/SFTP
connectivity. In accordance with LGPL-2.1, the paramiko source code is
available at the URL above. Users may replace the bundled paramiko with a
modified version by reinstalling from source and rebuilding the portable
